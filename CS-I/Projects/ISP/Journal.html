(Software) Development Engineer

A software development engineer is in charge of testing software, developing, and designing applications.These engineers make a multitude of products such as apps, websites, or computer program. They can work in various industries with experience in mechanical engineering or biotech.

QA Engineer

Quality Assurance engineers form tests to identify issues is software projects. They may analyze bugs and mistakes made before a product launch and document and review them. They are responsible for reporting results and collaborating with to fix program errors. A bachelors degree in computer science is necessary for this job.

Project Manager

Project Managers organize, planning and executing resources to bring a project together. They work on specific budgets and schedules. These managers work to market, lead teams, communicate stakeholder. They often run marketing campaign and document progress, assessing risks, or develop a computer system.

Product Manager

Product Managers are responsible for delivering a product and formulate business objectives. This job involves collaberating with teams to conduct market research, evaluate customers desires. They contribute to develop and launch the product by also organizing pricing, sales efforts and marketing.

Release Engineer

Release Engineers are responsible for testing and building software releases. This job involves the release process and setting up testing for projects. They often collaberate with QA teams to review the softwares stability. Release Engineers might manage source code control systems. Systems to guarantee that releases are properly documented.
